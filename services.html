These are our main services:

M-pesa Intergration

C2B Service

C2B = Customer to business

This are the daily to daily activities achieved by use of paybill and and till number such as payment of items.

B2C Service

B2C = Business to customer

This enables business to send money directly to a list of phone number from their bulk account. This is a convenient service to pay salaries to many staff to reduce time and error in doing this one by one.

B2B Service

B2B = Business to business

This enables business to pay other businesses. It's the same as B2C but instead of sending money to a personal phone number, you sent to a paybill or till number.

Google-pay Intergration

Google-pay for non-commercial

This includes intergrating payment in sites that do not sell products online or simply put non e-commerce. For example you are a mechanic or chef and you offer your services online therefore this could be the best suited for that purpose.

Google-pay for business

Payment gateway option for your Ecommerce business. This is best suited for businesses selling their products online. They include online stores and the related business.

Paypal Intergration

Paypal interface buttons

This invloves optionally customizing a payment button. The payment button's code snippet fitted to your website. However this intergration has a condition, only when a business owner sells only a few products on their site.

Paypal custom intergration

This is the use of a cart upload command to create a custom shopping cart experience. This applies a website which sells large number of products. In this case no third-party shopping cart is used.

Other Services
