Cryptocurrency exchanges

A cryptocurrency exchange is a marketplace for buying and selling cryptocurrencies. They give you accounts where you can generate various order types to buy, sell, and speculate in the cryptocurrency market.

Cryptocurrency exchanges

How do they crypto exchanges work?

Simply put cryptocurrency exchange makes it simple to swap one cryptocurrency for another “a crypto-to-crypto exchange” or fiat cash for cryptocurrencies “a fiat-to-crypto exchange”. When you buy or transfer digital assets the exchanges provide a wallet. There are 2 types of wallets namely: hot wallet and cold wallet which help one to store their assets.

Don’t worry about the vocabularies you have just encountered. I have explained all of their meaning down below.



What is fiat to crypto exchange?

This means that both fiat-crypto and crypto-crypto trading pairings are supported by fiat-to-crypto exchange. Users can fund their accounts with regular money through bank transfers, credit card payments, or other means.

What is crypto-to-crypto exchange ?

This is where one can exchange their cryptocurrency for other cryptocurrencies, such as coins or tokens not available on fiat-crypto exchanges. Traditional currencies and payment methods are not supported by crypto-to-crypto exchange.



What is a hot wallet??

Cold wallets are a way of holding cryptocurrency online. Hot wallets are connected to the internet through your computer or phone. Most crypto exchanges use this type of wallets although there are some that implement the two types of wallet storage for example Bitstamp.

Main disadvantage of hot wallet: The security breaches of private keys which kept online and in the wallet's browser. This concludes that they are prone cyber-attacks and assets stolen.

Solution to cyber attack risk: To reduce cybersecurity risks, hot wallet users should only retain a small amount of their cryptocurrency funds on these services.

What is a cold wallet?

Cold wallets are a way of holding cryptocurrency tokens totally offline and mostly is by use of hardware wallets. This way the security risk of being hacked are greatly minimized.

This type of storage gives you full control and security over your private keys and encryptions, it ensures you aren't affected by third party liabilities. Check the best cold storage options here

Disadvantage of using cold storage:It takes a lot of time to do a single transaction and cannot be used publicly.

Types of Crypto exchanges

There are 2 types of crypto exchanges that exist namely:
  • Centralized exchanges (CEX)
  • Decentralized exchanges (DEX)

Centralized exchanges

This is where an institution manages centralized cryptocurrency exchanges (CEX). Centralized exchanges make it simple to begin trading cryptocurrencies by allowing customers to transfer fiat cash, such as dollars, directly into crypto. The vast bulk of cryptocurrency trading occurs on controlled exchanges.

Characteristics of Centralized exchanges:
  • They are governed and licensed
  • They support a wide range of cryptocurrencies
  • Some allow you to earn cryptocurrency
  • Crypto-collateralized loans are available
  • Liquidity is lower than on other major global exchanges
  • Exchange institution holds the crypto traded on its platform

Decentralized exchanges

This is where digital currency exchange that enables users to purchase cryptocurrency through direct, peer-to-peer cryptocurrency transactions on an internet platform without the need of a middleman. Anyone who wants to join a DEX network can certify transactions, similar to how bitcoin blockchains operate.

Characteristics of decentralized exchanges:
  • They are not user friendly
  • DEXs provide varying levels of security, privacy, and efficiency from which crypto traders can choose.
  • Users of decentralized exchanges do not need to transfer their assets to a third party
  • Users have full control over their digital assets and are free to broadcast their portfolio to potential buyers
  • They don’t always support fiat to crypto transactions
  • You must either already hold cryptocurrency or utilize a centralized exchange to obtain cryptocurrency for usage on a DEX.
  • Direct peer-to-peer trades meaning allows users to buy and sell cryptocurrency without involving in a third party
  • Government guidelines, restrictions, and system monitoring are not present
